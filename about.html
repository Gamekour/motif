About me


Hi, I'm Seth Lewis(otherwise known as Motif.) I like to take up a lot of creative projects in various fields, mostly computer and machine related. My main motivations for my tech projects are usually

A. because I have a cool idea
B. because I want something custom-made
or C. because the actual thing is too expensive.

Other small things about me include my love for 80s and 90s related styles/music, my upmost appreciation of capybaras, and my 300 song playlist of nothing but the Family Guy intro on repeat.
I was also a lifeguard once. For some reason.

And yes, I make games. Talking about game design and specific elements of 3d graphics/physics is one of my favorite ways to annoy my friends. I stand by the fact that hypercasuals are hardly games as much as they are drugs, that a good game developer interacts with their community, and that The Stanley Parable is probably the best thing to have ever graced the Steam marketplace.
